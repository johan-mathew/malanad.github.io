Frequently Asked Questions

1. Where is Malanad Plantations located?+
Malanad has farms in Idukki and Kambam where we cultivate our delicious fruits and products.
2. What is our aim?+
Our aim is to provide nutritious yet delicious fruit products that are made from the fruits grown in our own plantations, with no artificial ingredients.
3.How do we manufacture our products?+
Handpicked fresh fruits from the farms undergo a special process to make the syrup, which is then bottled to be transported.
4.How are Malanad Plantation products different from the other products in the same industry?+
We follow sustainable agricultural practices, use mostly natural fertilisers or herbicides and use the least minimum amount of preservatives as the added sugar and the fruits themselves have the ability to preserve themselves
5.How to purchase from us?+
Yuo can contact us via phone or email id to make bulk orders, and our products are available at stores and supermarkets all over Kerala including Lulu, Navya Bakery,etc.
